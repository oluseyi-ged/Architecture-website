ShelterCrafts Consultants

Sheltercrafts Consultants Limited is an architecture, urban design and product management firm founded in 1999 and based in Lagos, Nigeria.
Sheltercrafts Consultants is a design practice with expertise in all phase of project development. From pre-design, design, contract decomentation to project implementation, we are committed to bringing up innovative designs that would lead to a better built environment.

We have since inception handled various Residential, Industrial and Commercial Projects.
At Sheltercrafts, understanding the uniqueness of every client as well as every project, we consistently strive to make the vision of every client a reality.
